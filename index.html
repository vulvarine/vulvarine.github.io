Welcome

Welcome to my professional website. I am Marie Biedermann, a graduate Master's student at the Alpe-Adria University of Klagenfurtn in Game Studies and Engineering.

About Me

Currently I am writing my thesis about pseudo-haptic feedback in digital card games while also developing a prototype in order to find the effects of pseudo-haptic feedback on player performance. Before, I graduated in English and American Studies (BA 2020) in Graz at the Karl-Franzens University. My first publication was a paper about an indie game Gato Roboto and its moral boundaries and technological invention during war. After this I, with a team, published a work-in-progress paper about the HEXAD player types in game jam populations by the CHI PLAY Conference 2023. On top of that, I participated in the development of eight games, that can be found on my itch.io page (https://vulvarine.itch.io/).
